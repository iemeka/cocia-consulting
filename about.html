Our emerging economy has increasing need for tailored financial solutions, a task that is growing in complexity. Meet the Team
  • MR. CHRISTIAN OGOCHUKWU AKAIGWE

Aprofessional banker with twenty five years experience from First Bank of Nigeria Plc. He has been involved in all aspects of branch operations, business development, public relations, project appraisal and monitoring. In branch operations, he performed senior functions like Head, Retail & Marketing, Head, Cash & Treasury Management amongst others. At different times, he was appointed to these senior positions in the bank: Public Relations Officer (East), Credit Analyst, Manager Mortgages & Head, Lending & Admin in Consumer Banking Dept. He voluntarily resigned from the bank in January 2008 to join Conau Group in February 2008, where he was appointed Group Head, Investment & Portfolio. He saw to acquisition and consolidation of the following businesses within the group; Universal Insurance Plc, African Alliance Insurance Plc, Ghana Life Insurance Ltd, Frenchies Ltd, Axiom Air Ltd, Molit Hotels & Universal Hotels Ltd.

He resigned from the group in 2012 to establish Esteem Financial Consultancy Services Ltd where he served as Chairman, before finally setting up COCIA Consulting in 2014.

He holds HND in Banking & Finance from Federal Polytechnic Oko and an MBA degree from Ambrose Alli University, Ekpoma. He also holds the professional qualifications of Fellow, Institute of Professional Recruitment Consultants, Associates (ACIB) from the Chartered Institute of Bankers of Nigeria and Member, Chartered Institute of Cost Management (MCICM).

  • MRS. IJEOMA CELESTINA AKAIGWE

Ijeoma is an Economist. Her working experience started in 1997 at the Banking Supervision Department of Central Bank of Nigeria where she served as a Corp Member.Between the year 2000 and 2005, she taught Economics at Command Day Secondary School, Oshodi, Lagos. From the year 2007 to 2014, she worked as a director in Hendon Bureau de Change Ltd.

She is currently a director of COCIA Concepts Ltd. She obtained a BSc. Economics from the University of Nigeria in 1996 and MSc. Economics from the University of Lagos in 2005.

She is happily married with children. Ijeoma is a believer in doing things right. She loves reading and listening to good music.

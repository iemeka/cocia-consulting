OUR SERVICE PORTFOLIO

Our service bouquet includes but not limited to the following:

CORPORATE SERVICES & OPERATIONS

Banks and other financial institutions have the growing challenge of expanding the breadth and depth of their service coverage at little additional cost. Outsourcing, Franchising and readily employed.

We come in as Agents in the service chain in the management of:
- Cash and Teller operations
- Payment and service channels
- Reconciliations
- Human capital management etc

FINANCIAL EDUCATION AND TRAINING

We are constantly updating our training packs towards addressing gaps noticed in contemporary banking as well as the noticeably low financial literacy in non banking groups for whom appropriate learning programs have been structured.

We conduct training in all areas of banking and run financial literacy programs for non banking groups. We develop operations/service manuals for banking and related services as well engage in research and financial publications.

LOAN FINANCE AND ADMINISTRATION SERVICES

- Packaging of loans including due diligence on parties. Prompt execution of financing mandates leveraging on strong contacts with local, offshore and multilateral financiers
- Ensuring compliance by parties to loan covenants and indentures
- Arranging guarantees where required

Remediation and Arbitration services

- Loan Recoveries
- Investigation and mutual Resolution of financial disputes

CORPORATE FINANCE AND ADVISORY SERVICES

- Advise on funding requirements and optimal financial structure, financial restructuring and workouts
- Raising finance—equity finance, loan finance, quasi loan including floating private placements and venture capital for start ups, long term loans, debentures and bonds and general financial advisory.
